### Plaque Assay

* The plaque assay is a widely used method to measure virus titer, which is the amount of virus in a sample.
* Renato Dulbecco who developed the plaque procedure in 1952.

* [Plaque assay](http://www.virology.ws/2009/07/06/detecting-viruses-the-plaque-assay/)
* only plates containing between 10 and 100 plaques are counted
